# FAQs

## Who can add applications?
Users with the **Developer** or **Admin** role.

## Can I disable specific scan types?
Yes—this can be controlled per application.

## How often are scans run?
Scans are triggered manually or via CI/CD integrations.

## Are vulnerabilities removed after resolution?
Resolved issues remain in history for compliance.

## Can AppSec+ notify my team in Slack?
Yes—via Slack connector or webhook events.
