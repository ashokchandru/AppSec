# Policy Center

Policies control how AppSec+ responds to detected issues.

## Policy Types
- Blocking policies (fail builds)
- Monitoring policies (report-only)
- Application-specific policies

## Creating a Policy
1. Go to **Policies**
2. Click **New Policy**
3. Define:
   - Conditions (severity, tags, app groups)
   - Action (block / monitor)
4. Save

## Policy Violations
A violation occurs when scan results meet policy conditions.
